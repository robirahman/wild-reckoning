# Wild Reckoning

A high-fidelity wildlife survival simulator. Navigate the complex struggle for genetic fitness as one of 10 unique species across diverse biomes â€” from the Arctic Circle to the East African Savanna.

Navigate predators, parasites, weather, and ecosystem shifts in a turn-based, narrative-driven experience.

## Key Features

- **10 Playable Species:** Including White-Tailed Deer, African Elephant, Polar Bear, Monarch Butterfly, Honeybee, and more.
- **Deep Biological Simulation:** Multi-stage parasites, injury healing, seasonal thermoregulation, and nutrition systems.
- **Evolution & Lineage:** Accumulate genetic mutations and pass trait biases down to future generations.
- **Map Navigation:** Explore regional maps with dynamic resources and hazards.
- **Ecosystem Web:** Experience trophic cascades where your actions and environmental shifts impact predator-prey populations.
